PokerStata

PokerStata is a poker calculator that can evaluate equity of selected hands against specified range and certain categories of strength hands. Save selected range, borad and hand. Use one of 10 range type presets e.g. "Sklansky".

Spectrum

Use the slider to select desired range
or use “fast range” buttons.

Storage

Save the current analysis now
and continue in the future.

Statistics

Calculate the equity of your hand
against the specified range.

Available for iPhone and Android.
